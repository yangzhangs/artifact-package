# RQ1: Required Conditions Imposed by Permissive AIPs

This table summarizes the condition categories identified through manual labeling of all 540 conditional permissive AIPs. One AIP may contain multiple conditions; percentages need not add up to 100.

| ID | Condition | Count | % |
|----|-----------|-------|---|
| C1 | Need human review | 438 | 81.1% |
| C2 | Need explicit disclosure | 205 | 38.0% |
| C3 | Need to follow AI-specific rules | 124 | 23.0% |
| C4 | Need to meet quality standards | 105 | 19.4% |
| C5 | Need to supplement evidence | 43 | 8.0% |

**Notes:**
- Total permissive AIPs: 544 (of which 540 impose at least one condition; 4 impose no conditions)
- Total prohibited AIPs: 71
- Overall stance distribution: 544 permissive (88.6%) vs. 71 prohibited (11.4%) out of 615 confirmed AIPs

**Condition descriptions:**

- **C1. Human review (81.1%):** Require that a human review and take responsibility for AI contributions before submission.
- **C2. Explicit disclosure (38.0%):** Require some form of disclosure about AI use in the PR or commit.
- **C3. Follow AI-specific rules (23.0%):** Impose rules governing specific aspects of AI use (e.g., quantitative limits, permissible scopes).
- **C4. Meet quality standards (19.4%):** Require that AI contributions meet the same quality bar as human-written code.
- **C5. Supplement evidence (8.0%):** Require contributors to provide additional evidence of correctness when using AI (e.g., prompts used, tests).
